St Matthew's Carol Service 2017

This site contains all of the resources that you will need in order to learn your parts for the 2017 St. Matthew's Carols Service.

If you have any questions about the service, please email worship@st-matts.org.uk.

Choir packs

These choir packs contain all of the music for the service in a single PDF. Simply click on the link for your vocal part to download your copy!
